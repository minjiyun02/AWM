Welcome to the
AWM Student Chapter at UC Davis!

We are an official chapter of the Association for Women in Mathematics, and we received a Community Service Award at the "Silver" level from the UC Davis Internship and Career Center in recognition of our efforts for the 2018-19 academic year. The navigation bar at the top links to detailed information about our organization, including upcoming events, meeting times, and contact information. This website is also home to a growing list of resources for women in math. Please contact us at awmatucd@gmail.com or awm_exec@math.ucdavis.edu with any additions, suggestions, or other feedback!
